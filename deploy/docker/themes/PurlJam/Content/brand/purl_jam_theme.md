
# Purl Jam — Brand & Website Theme Guide

## Brand Overview
**Purl Jam** is a modern fiber studio focused on knitting, crochet, and creative community.
The brand balances warmth and approachability with clean, contemporary design.

**Brand Personality**
- Modern, calm, creative
- Welcoming to beginners, respected by experienced makers
- Community-oriented (classes, jams, workshops)
- Boutique quality, not rustic or cutesy

---

## Logo System
Included logo assets:
- Primary logo (icon + wordmark)
- Icon-only mark (for favicon, social, tags)
- Horizontal logo
- Monochrome logo

Usage guidelines:
- Prefer sage green on light backgrounds
- Use charcoal or black for text-heavy layouts
- Maintain generous white space

---

## Color Palette

**Primary**
- Wool Cream: `#F5F1EA`
- Sage Green: `#8FA99B`

**Accent**
- Soft Charcoal: `#2F2F2F`

**Usage**
- Cream for backgrounds
- Sage for brand highlights, buttons, icons
- Charcoal for text and contrast

---

## Typography

**Headings**
- Modern serif (e.g. Canela, Playfair, Cormorant)
- Slightly increased letter spacing
- Calm, confident tone

**Body**
- Humanist sans-serif (e.g. Inter, Source Sans, Lato)
- High readability, friendly feel

---

## UI Components

**Buttons**
- Rounded corners
- Sage background with cream text
- Subtle hover darkening

**Cards**
- Soft shadows
- Cream backgrounds
- Generous padding

**Navigation**
- Minimal
- Clear hierarchy
- Shop and Classes as primary CTAs

---

## Imagery & Icons

**Photography**
- Natural light
- Neutral textiles
- Hands-in-process shots
- No harsh colors

**Icons**
- Thin line style
- Yarn, hooks, needles, shop, calendar

---

## Website Sections (Suggested)
- Hero: Welcome + Shop CTA
- Featured Yarn
- Upcoming Classes
- About the Sisters
- Newsletter Signup
- Instagram / Community Gallery

---

## Tone for Copy
- Friendly
- Encouraging
- Knowledgeable
- Inclusive

Example:
> “Pull up a chair and stitch with us.”

---

## Technical Notes for Codex
- Favor semantic HTML
- CSS variables for colors
- Mobile-first layout
- Accessible contrast ratios
